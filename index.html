Welcome

This is the home of the course Evidence-based Decision Making, FS2020, at the Faculty of Psychology, University of Basel.

The behavioral sciences, such as psychology and economics, have developed a number of techniques to gain insights from empirical evidence to help individuals, groups, and policy makers make better decisions in disparate domains, including management, marketing, and health-care. This course aims to provide a general introduction to evidence-based decision making, including some of the techniques that have emerged in the past years to help decision makers make informed choices. You can find out more about the contents of this course in the Syllabus.

The course involves a few classroom experiments and exercises and students get a chance to work with the corresponding data. Students interested in participating in these components may want to visit the R Installation page for some background information concerning R.

Information and instructions about the classroom exercises will be made available throughout the semester in the Exercises page.

Looking forward to seeing you in class!
